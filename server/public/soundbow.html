DRAW YOUR SOUND

To include you, the audience, into Tampere Flute Festival we are asking you to draw us your sound. There are two sets of sounds (1 and 2) and you can move the strings on the screen however you want. When you are done drawing press “SEND” and the sound will be recorded and sent to us. If you aren't happy with what you hear you can always “RESET” and try again. We will include the sounds sent to us in our festival in Tampere in 2022.

SEND THE SOUND?

Each user is limited to one sound. When you are satisfied with the sound, press “SEND” and the sound will be recorded and sent to us. If you aren't happy with what you hear you can return back to drawing your contribution by "BACK" and try again.

After clicking "SEND", we'll verify that you are human with a Google's reCAPTCHA test.
