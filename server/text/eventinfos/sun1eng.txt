Four flutists from around the world compete in the final round of the 2022 TFF Young Artist Competition. Finalists will each perform a 25-minute program displaying a wide range of musical styles. Winners will be announced at the closing ceremonies taking place in the Gala Concert. Winners of the 2022 Young Artist Competition Category B and Category C, the Piccolo Competition and the Flute Lovers Competition will be announced in a short ceremony at the end of the final round of the Young Artist Competition Category A.
