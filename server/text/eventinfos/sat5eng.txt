Selected flutists, 18 years and younger, have the opportunity to learn and perform in a masterclass for Jenny Villanen, solo piccolo of the Helsinki Philharmonic.
Masterclass participants:
Taimi Lajunen: Vivaldi Piccolo Concerto in C
Matilda Neva: Marttinen Ilmatar
Lumi Seikkula: Chopin Nocturne
Matilda Suova: Marttinen Ilmatar
Tommi Tang: Bach Badinerie
Lotta Tuominen: Popp Venäläinen kansanlaulu
