14-year old prodigy flutist Fabian Egger won First Prize in the 2021 TFF Young Artist Competition Category B. A year later, he was accepted into the esteemed studio of Andrea Liebernecht at the Hochschule für Musik und Tanz München, as the youngest student to ever be admitted. Young talent Egger, presents an exciting program featuring Prokofiev’s Sonata for flute and piano, Rae’s Sonatina for flute and piano and his very own composition, dedicated to this year’s festival, Icy Times. 
Piano: Hanna-Leena Savolainen
