This short and colorful program will delight your ears with the magical sounds of the piccolo. Jenny Villanen, solo piccolo of the Helsinki Philharmonic, includes in her program Kuhlau’s Introduction und Variationen über ein Thema aus Carl Maria von Webers “Euryanthe”’ op. 63, Smith’s Song Flight for solo piccolo and Harberg’s Sonata for piccolo.
Piano: Viktor Pellia
