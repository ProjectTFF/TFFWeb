Turning the spotlight on the icons of American and French flute music. International soloist Beatriz Macías and principal flutist of the Finnish National Opera & Ballet Alexis Roman come together, to perform Burton’s Sonatina for flute and piano, Widor’s Suite for flute and piano, Maxfield’s world premiere of Sunflowers for flute and piano, and Berlioz’s Trio of the Young Ishmaelites for two flutes and piano.
Piano: Viktor Pellia
