Three selected flutists perform in a masterclass for world-renowned flutist Sébastian Jacot, principal flutist of the Gewandhaus Orchestra in Leipzig.
Masterclass participants:
Fabian Egger: Mozart Concerto in G Major, 1st mvt
Stella Michael J.S. Bach E Major Sonata, 1st and 2mtv
Dain Song Debussy Afternoon of a Faun, Ravel Daphnis et Chloé and Brahms Symphony No. 4 orchestral excerpts
