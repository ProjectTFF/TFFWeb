Pianist Hanna-Leena Savolainen works as an accompanist and pedagogue at TAMK Music Academy and Pirkanmaan Music School. In addition to these educational posts, Savolainen is an active chamber musician and frequently performs with various ensembles such as the Tampere Philharmonic Orchestra. Savolainen holds a Master of Music degree in Music Performance from the Sibelius Academy of Music, where she studied under professor Erik T. Tawaststjerna and Hanna Aho-Oikkonen. She has performed as a soloist with the Finnish Radio Symphony Orchestra and Sinfonia Lahti, as well as won numerous awards in competitions such as the prestigious Leevi Madetoja Piano Competition. Savolainen is co-founder of the Kitee Chamber Concerts, a chamber music festival created together with her musical family.
