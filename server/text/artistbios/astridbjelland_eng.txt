24-year-old Norwegian flutist Astrid Bjelland won First Prize in the 2021 Tampere Flute Fest Young Artist Competition and was selected to participate in the 10th Kobe International Flute Competition. Astrid has performed as a soloist in Scandinavia, Germany, Spain and China, and has worked with several professional orchestras in Scandinavia, including the Danish National Symphony Orchestra as Co-Principal Flutist. Astrid is currently pursuing her Master’s Degree at the Royal Danish Academy of Music in Copenhagen with prof. Ulla Miilmann. She has also studied with prof. Mario Caroli at the Hochschule für Musik Freiburg, and has performed in masterclasses for Emmanuel Pahud, Emily Beynon, Vincent Lucas and Robert Langevin.
